Functional Programming Brno

Functional Programming Brno (FPBrno or FPB for short) is for everyone interested in functional programming who happens to be in Brno or nearby areas. Activities include but are not limited to talks and discussions.

We have a mailing list (online archive) that you can sign-up to simply by sending an email to fpbrno+subscribe@googlegroups.com (even an empty email will do).
