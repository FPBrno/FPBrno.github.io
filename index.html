Functional Programming Brno

Functional Programming Brno (FPBrno or FPB for short) is for all people with interest in functional programming that happen to be in Brno or nearby areas. Activities include but are not limited to talks and discussions.

We have a mailing list (online archive) that you can sign-up to simply by sending mail to fpbrno+subscribe@googlegroups.com (even empty mail will do).
